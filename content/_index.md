I build fixes for people stuck doing things the hard way — operations teams, plant managers, finance analysts. Sometimes that person is me. Process, hardware, or software — the problem picks and I figure out the rest with whatever's lying around.

My day job is at a manufacturing company, where I work across operations, sales, marketing, and anywhere else the work needs improving. I write code, solve production problems, and make messy data useful to people who actually need it. The project I'm proudest of started as a dashboard and became something three facilities run their daily operations on.
